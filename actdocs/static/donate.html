You can help the Perl Toolchain Summit too

Why donate?

A typical PTS costs about 25000 € (hotel rooms 25 %, travels 35 %, venue 15 %, food and beverages 15 %).

You're a Perl user. You appreciate the quality of the language. You think it worth to invest some money to have help these people make Perl and CPAN even better...

The money that will not be used for the Perl Toolchain Summit 2024 will be donated for the Perl Toolchain Summit 2024 (or the next one).

Organizers track accurately the budget. You can ask the incomes/expenses of the former editions, if you're interested in.

How?

You can donate to the hackathon in EUR (€), USD ($) and GBP (£).
